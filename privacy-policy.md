# Privacy Policy

**MojiGen**
Last updated: March 30, 2026

---

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to MojiGen ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application MojiGen (the "App"). Please read this policy carefully. If you disagree with its terms, please discontinue use of the App.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **User-generated content**: Text prompts and images you submit to generate stickers or emojis. These are sent to our AI processing pipeline (fal.ai) and are not stored permanently on our servers beyond what is necessary for generation.
- **Account information**: We use anonymous Firebase Authentication. No name, email address, or personal identifier is required to use the App.

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Usage data**: We collect anonymous data about how you interact with the App (features used, generation counts, session duration) to improve our service.
- **Device information**: Device type, operating system version, and app version for diagnostic and analytics purposes.
- **Crash reports**: Anonymous crash logs via Firebase Crashlytics to help us fix bugs.

### 2.3 Purchase Information
- **Subscription and payment data**: Processed entirely by Apple via the App Store. We do not collect or store your credit card or payment details. We receive only anonymized transaction confirmations via RevenueCat.

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide, operate, and maintain the App
- Generate AI stickers and emojis based on your inputs
- Process and manage your subscription or credit purchases
- Monitor and analyze usage to improve user experience
- Detect and prevent technical issues or abuse

---

## 4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share anonymous or aggregated data with:

- **fal.ai**: Our AI generation infrastructure provider. Images and text prompts you submit are processed by fal.ai to generate output. Please review [fal.ai's privacy policy](https://fal.ai/privacy) for details.
- **Google Firebase**: Used for anonymous authentication, database storage, and crash reporting. [Firebase Privacy Policy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy).
- **RevenueCat**: Used to manage subscriptions and in-app purchases. [RevenueCat Privacy Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy).
- **Apple App Store**: Handles all payment processing. [Apple Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

We may also disclose your information if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

---

## 5. Data Retention

- **Generated content**: Images and prompts processed through our AI pipeline are not stored beyond the immediate generation session.
- **Anonymous usage data**: Retained for up to 12 months for analytics purposes.
- **Account data**: Retained until you delete your account or request deletion.

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

MojiGen is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.
